Common Questions

What areas do you serve?

We proudly serve Imus, Cavite, and the wider Calabarzon region. For larger projects, we're open to discussing service beyond this area. Please contact us to confirm availability in your location.

How often should I have my AC unit maintained?

For optimal performance and longevity, we recommend annual maintenance. If you use your AC frequently or live in a dusty environment, consider more frequent servicing.

What are the warning signs my AC needs repair?

Watch for reduced airflow, unusual noises or smells, leaks, higher energy bills, or inconsistent cooling. Contact us immediately if you notice any of these.

Do you provide financing options?

Yes, we offer flexible financing solutions to make your comfort investment easier. Contact us to discuss available plans and find the best option for you.

What types of air conditioning systems do you service?

We service a wide range of AC systems, including split-type units, window-type units, central air conditioning systems, and ductless mini-split systems. Our technicians are trained to handle various makes and models.

How long does a typical AC installation take?

The duration of an AC installation depends on several factors, such as the type of unit and the complexity of the installation process. A typical split-type installation might take a few hours, while central AC installations can take longer. We'll provide you with an estimated timeframe before starting the work.

What is included in a standard AC maintenance service?

A standard AC maintenance service typically includes cleaning or replacing air filters, cleaning the condenser and evaporator coils, checking refrigerant levels, inspecting electrical connections, lubricating moving parts, and ensuring the overall system is functioning efficiently.

Are your technicians licensed and insured?

Yes, all our technicians are fully licensed, insured, and highly trained to provide professional and reliable air conditioning services. Your peace of mind is our priority.

What should I do if my AC is making strange noises?

If your AC is making unusual noises such as banging, grinding, hissing, or clicking, it's important to turn off the unit and contact us for service. These noises could indicate a mechanical issue that needs immediate attention.

How can I improve the energy efficiency of my AC system?

You can improve your AC's energy efficiency by regularly changing air filters, ensuring proper insulation in your home, using a programmable thermostat, keeping windows and doors closed when the AC is running, and scheduling regular maintenance to keep your unit running optimally. Upgrading to a more energy-efficient model can also make a significant difference.
